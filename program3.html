About us

MEA Engineering College, Vengoor, established in 2002, is a self-financing engineering institution located in Malappuram district, Kerala. Affiliated to APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by AICTE, the college offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering and technology streams. Set in a scenic campus surrounded by greenery, MEAEC provides modern infrastructure, including well-equipped labs, hostels, and transport facilities. The college has earned NAAC accreditation and aims to deliver quality technical education at an affordable cost. It is known for its academic focus, student support services, and active involvement in community development.
